About

    We are students at the École polytechnique with an interest computer science, specifically algorithmics and data science

    We aim to promote computer and data science at the Ecole Polytechnique. To achieve that, we

  • train students who apply to programming competitions and challenges,
  • train students for code interviews with the support of both teachers at the IT department and companies,
  • organise talks and conferences given by experts in the field,
  • do consulting and data science work for companies as well as nonprofit enterprises,
  • provide students with computing resources and training data,
  • work with companies to offer students lab visits, material for Group Science Projects or internships

For any enquiry, please get in touch with us.
